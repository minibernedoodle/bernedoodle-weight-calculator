How Big Will My Mini Bernedoodle Get?

The adult size of a Mini Bernedoodle varies depending on genetics, diet, and exercise. Typically, they weigh between 18 to 45 pounds when fully grown.

Factors That Affect Mini Bernedoodle Weight

  • Parent Size – A Mini Bernedoodle with a Toy Poodle parent will likely be smaller than one with a Miniature Poodle parent.
  • Generation Type – F1, F1B, and F2 Bernedoodles have different growth patterns.
  • Diet & Nutrition – A high-quality diet rich in protein, healthy fats, and vitamins is essential.
  • Exercise & Activity Level – Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight.
